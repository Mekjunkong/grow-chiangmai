# Grow Chiang Mai Kanban Missions

Status snapshot created from the landing-page critique.

## Mission 1 — Founding demand proof

- Status: Doing now
- Value: prove demand before cafe/buildout spend.
- Acceptance criteria:
  - 40 serious waitlist leads or 20 paid managed-plot commitments are tracked.
  - Each lead records package interest, preferred area, visit rhythm, and care preference.
  - No payment is requested until bed/location/care details are personally confirmed.
- Dependencies: real waitlist backend before public promotion.

## Mission 2 — Customer clarity conversion pass

- Status: In progress
- Value: make the landing page feel like a customer offer, not only a business plan.
- Acceptance criteria:
  - Hero explains what the member gets in one readable paragraph.
  - Primary CTA says no payment today.
  - Membership cards explain managed vs DIY clearly.
  - Five-step journey explains waitlist to harvest.

## Mission 3 — Local proof pack

- Status: Next
- Value: make the concept feel real in Chiang Mai.
- Acceptance criteria:
  - Add real land/access/water/parking/toilet/caretaker photos when available.
  - Add sample crop calendar for Thai herbs/vegetables.
  - Add camera-angle/privacy examples.
  - Keep wording careful: service membership, organic-method, no guaranteed harvest.

## Mission 4 — Real lead operations

- Status: Blocked until backend choice
- Value: make the waitlist usable outside a static prototype.
- Acceptance criteria:
  - Choose Airtable, Google Sheets, Supabase, or Vercel API route.
  - Store every valid form submission server-side.
  - Show confirmation to the member and notify the owner.
  - Export/filter leads by plan, area, and care preference.

## Mission 5 — QA and deploy evidence

- Status: Review
- Value: ship only after proof.
- Acceptance criteria:
  - Static build passes.
  - HTML parses without errors.
  - Desktop and mobile screenshots checked.
  - CTA and waitlist validation verified.
