Launch pricing logic

Charge for care, camera trust, and a comfortable place to arrive.

Chiang Mai already has affordable coworking and farm workshops. Grow Chiang Mai can sit above a day activity because members get a real bed, ongoing care, camera visibility, and a place to return.

Farm learning benchmark

Local organic farm cooking classes commonly sit around THB 1,200 to THB 1,500 per person for a one-day experience.

AC comfort benchmark

Chiang Mai coworking day access often sits around THB 150 to THB 300, with monthly hot desks around THB 2,500 to THB 4,500.

Camera cost reality

Occasional live viewing can use meaningful mobile data, so camera access should be included in premium plans or priced as a paid add-on.

Clear before you join

Questions a serious member will ask.

The answers stay practical because the first farm is still a pilot.

Are members renting Thai land?

No. The safer structure is a Thai-operated farm membership and service. Members receive access, care, learning, updates, and harvest options. Legal wording is kept clear before any paid membership is confirmed.

Is it certified organic?

Not yet. We use organic methods, natural inputs, compost, and careful growing practices. Certified organic is only claimed after real certification and recordkeeping.

Can I watch my plot on camera?

Yes, that is the main premium feature. Live Plot View should point only at the member bed, with no audio, no visitor surveillance, signage on site, and one private access link per plot.

What happens if my crop fails?

Farming has weather, insects, and timing risk. Managed members should receive field notes, replanting plans when possible, and honest seasonal expectations rather than guaranteed harvest claims.
